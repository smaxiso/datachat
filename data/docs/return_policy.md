# Return Policy

## Overview
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging.

## Conditions
- Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt.
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached.
- Proof of purchase is required.

## Process
1. Initiate a return via the user dashboard.
2. Print the prepaid shipping label.
3. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping center.

## Refunds
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The amount will be credited back to the original payment method.

## Exceptions
- Final sale items cannot be returned.
- Personalized items are non-refundable.
