# Shipping Information

## Delivery Options
We offer the following shipping methods:

- **Standard Shipping**: 3-5 business days. Free for orders over $50.
- **Express Shipping**: 1-2 business days. Flat rate of $15.
- **International Shipping**: 7-14 business days. Rates calculated at checkout.

## Tracking
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.

## Delays
Please note that shipping times may be affected by holidays or severe weather conditions.
